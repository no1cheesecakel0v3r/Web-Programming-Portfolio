"Sweet Strawberry Cheesecake"

A heavenly combination of a very creamy cheesecake and fresh and yummy strawberries, perfect for any special occasion!
( or if you just have cravings at home! ğŸ“ )

Prep Time: 30 mins
Cook Time: 1 hour
Serves: 8-10

Ingredients

Ingredients for the Crust:
  • 2 cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
Ingredients for the Filling:
  • 24 oz softned cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of fresh sliced strawberries
  • Optional: Strawberry Jam

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325ÂºF / 165ÂºC. Mix the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter and sugar. Press into a 9 inch baking pan.
  2. Beat the cream cheese and sugar until they are smooth. Add eggs one at a time, mixing it in at a low speed.
  3. Add sour cream and vanilla extract together and mix well until it is blended.
  4. Pour half of the batter into the crust. Layer the some of the cut strawberries and then put the remaining batter on top. ( if you'd prefer strawberry jam instead, you could use that as a substitute for the middle of the cake. )
  5. Bake for about 1 hour or until the center is almost set. Remove it from the oven and use a knife to unstick the dessert of the rim of the pan.
  6. Let it cool, and then refridgerate it overnight.

Baking Tips

  • Make sure that all the ingredients are at room temperature before starting.
  • Don't overmix the batter to prevent cracks from forming.
  • Use fresh and ripe strawberries for the best flavours possible!
