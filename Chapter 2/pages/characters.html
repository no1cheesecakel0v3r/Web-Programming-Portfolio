Characters

The world of Final Fantasy XV is inhabited by a diverse cast of compelling characters, from the steadfast heroes to the complex villains. Below you'll find information about the main characters who shape the destiny of Eos.


Main Characters

Noctis Lucis Caelum

Crown Prince of Lucis | Main Protagonist

The sole heir to the Lucian throne, Noctis embarks on a journey to reclaim his kingdom after it falls to imperial forces. Though he can appear aloof and moody, he deeply cares for his friends and the people of Lucis. Noctis possesses the power to wield various weapons through the Armiger, a mystical arsenal passed down through the Lucian royal family.

As the Chosen King prophesied to save the world from darkness, Noctis must come to terms with his destiny and the sacrifices it demands. His growth from a somewhat reluctant prince to a determined king forms the emotional core of the story.

Gladiolus Amicitia

Shield of the King | Sworn Protector

Born into a family that has served as the Shield of the King for generations, Gladiolus (or "Gladio") takes his duty to protect Noctis very seriously. Large and imposing, he specializes in greatswords and acts as the group's powerhouse in battle.

Gladio can be stern and harsh, especially when he feels Noctis is not living up to his responsibilities, but this comes from a place of profound loyalty and dedication. His straightforward nature and unwavering resolve make him the backbone of the group.

Ignis Scientia

Royal Advisor | Tactician

Serving as Noctis's advisor, strategist, and caretaker, Ignis is intelligent, composed, and meticulous. He plans the group's tactics in battle, drives the Regalia, and even cooks their meals, creating new recipes from ingredients they find during their journey.

Ignis's unwavering dedication to Noctis is tested after he loses his eyesight in Altissia, forcing him to adapt to his new limitations while continuing to support his king. His sacrifice and resilience exemplify his character's depth and commitment.

Prompto Argentum

Crownsguard | Best Friend

Unlike the others, Prompto wasn't born into nobility or raised to serve the crown. He befriended Noctis during high school, worked to improve himself, and eventually earned a place in the Crownsguard through perseverance and loyalty.

Cheerful and upbeat, Prompto lightens the mood of the group with his jokes and enthusiasm. He's an amateur photographer who documents their journey through his camera. Later revelations about his origins as a child designed to become an MT (Magitek Trooper) add complexity to his character as he struggles with questions of identity and belonging.

Lunafreya Nox Fleuret

Oracle | Former Princess of Tenebrae

Known to most as Lady Luna, she is the youngest Oracle in history, blessed with the ability to commune with the gods and heal those afflicted by the Starscourge. Though she was meant to marry Noctis as part of a peace treaty, her duty extends far beyond political arrangements.

Dignified, compassionate, and resolute, Luna works tirelessly to help Noctis fulfill his destiny, awakening the Astrals so that he may forge covenants with them. Her devotion to her calling and to Noctis shapes much of the game's narrative and emotional impact.

Antagonists

Ardyn Izunia

Imperial Chancellor | Main Antagonist

The enigmatic chancellor of Niflheim, Ardyn appears charming and helpful at first, but his true motives remain obscured until late in the story. His flamboyant demeanor masks a deep-seated bitterness and thirst for vengeance.

Eventually revealed to be Ardyn Lucis Caelum, he was once a healer who absorbed daemons to save others, only to be rejected by the Crystal and immortalized in darkness. His complex history and motivations make him one of the most nuanced villains in the Final Fantasy series.

Emperor Iedolas Aldercapt

Emperor of Niflheim

The elderly ruler of the Niflheim Empire, Iedolas was once a visionary leader who sought to bring prosperity to his people. However, his obsession with obtaining the Crystal's power led him down a dark path, making him susceptible to Ardyn's manipulations.

As the story progresses, Iedolas becomes increasingly consumed by his desire for power, eventually succumbing to the Starscourge and transforming into a daemon.

Ravus Nox Fleuret

High Commander of the Imperial Army | Luna's Brother

Luna's older brother and former prince of Tenebrae, Ravus initially serves Niflheim despite his hatred for the empire that conquered his homeland. His complicated feelings toward Noctis—whom he blames for his mother's death and the fall of Tenebrae—drive much of his character arc.

Over time, Ravus's priorities shift as he realizes the empire's true nature and his sister's unwavering faith in Noctis. His journey from antagonist to reluctant ally showcases the game's theme of redemption.

Supporting Characters

Regis Lucis Caelum CXIII

King of Lucis | Noctis's Father

The 113th king of Lucis, Regis maintains a magical barrier around Insomnia through the power of the Crystal, though this takes a significant toll on his health. Knowing the prophecy that surrounds his son, he makes the painful decision to send Noctis away from the city before its fall.

Despite limited screen time, King Regis's love for his son and his kingdom resonates throughout the narrative, particularly in his final moments and his posthumous guidance.

Cor Leonis

Marshal of the Crownsguard

Known as "The Immortal" for his legendary combat prowess and survival skills, Cor serves as a mentor to Noctis and his friends after the fall of Insomnia. He guides them to the royal tombs where Noctis can claim the power of his ancestors.

Stoic and dutiful, Cor represents the old guard of Lucis, maintaining his loyalty to the crown even after the kingdom's fall.

Iris Amicitia

Gladiolus's Sister

The younger sister of Gladiolus, Iris harbors a crush on Noctis and provides emotional support to the group during their visit to Lestallum. Though young, she demonstrates the same courage and resilience that characterize her family.

In the World of Ruin, Iris becomes known as "The Daemon Slayer," fighting to protect survivors during the long night, showing her growth from a cheerful girl to a formidable warrior.

Cid Sophiar

Mechanic | Former Companion of King Regis

A gruff mechanic who once traveled with King Regis on his own journey, Cid now runs the Hammerhead service station with his granddaughter Cindy. He provides valuable upgrades to Noctis's weapons and the Regalia.

Though outwardly harsh, especially toward Noctis, Cid's criticism comes from concern and high expectations, as he sees Regis in his son.

Cindy Aurum

Mechanic at Hammerhead

Cid's granddaughter and an exceptional mechanic in her own right, Cindy maintains and upgrades the Regalia throughout the journey. Her sunny disposition and passion for automobiles make her a memorable character despite limited screen time.

Cindy represents the resilience of the people living outside the crown city, adapting to life under imperial rule while maintaining their independence and spirit.
